Macarona Bachamel

Description

Macarona Bechamel, a beloved Egyptian dish, is a delightful comfort food that combines layers of pasta, a rich and creamy béchamel sauce, and flavorful minced meat. Often compared to lasagna, this dish has its unique Egyptian twist, using penne or macaroni pasta and aromatic spices like cinnamon and nutmeg to season the meat layer. The dish is assembled with alternating layers of pasta, seasoned meat, and béchamel, then baked to golden perfection. Macarona Bechamel is a staple at family gatherings, celebrations, and special occasions in Egypt, offering a hearty and satisfying meal that reflects the warmth of Egyptian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g penne or macaroni pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for boiling the pasta)
  • A pinch of salt (for boiling water)
  • 500g minced beef (or lamb)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for sautéing)
  • 4 cups (1 liter) milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and white pepper (to taste)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese (optional, for a golden top)
  • 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs (optional, for texture)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the pasta and cook until al dente (firm to the bite).
    • Drain the pasta and toss with a tablespoon of olive oil to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare the meat filling:
    • Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
    • Stir in the tomato paste, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
    • Cook for 5-7 minutes until the mixture is well combined and aromatic. Set aside.
  4. Prepare the béchamel sauce:
    • Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    • Add the flour and whisk constantly for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
    • Gradually add the milk, whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
    • Add the nutmeg, salt, and white pepper to season.
    • Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens. Remove from heat.
    • (Optional) Stir in heavy cream for extra richness.
  5. Assemble the dish:
    • Spread a thin layer of béchamel sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Mix half of the cooked pasta with a portion of the béchamel sauce, ensuring it is well coated.
    • Layer the pasta mixture evenly in the dish.
    • Spread the meat filling on top of the pasta layer.
    • Mix the remaining pasta with more béchamel sauce and layer it over the meat.
    • Pour the remaining béchamel sauce on top, ensuring it covers the entire surface.
    • (Optional) Sprinkle shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese on top for a golden crust.
  6. Bake the dish:
    • Place the dish in the preheated oven.
    • Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbling.
    • Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
  7. Serve and enjoy your delicious Egyptian Macarona Bechamel!
